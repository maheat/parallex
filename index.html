Sapphire Dreams: Poetry under the Blue Skies

Let these verses transport you to the tranquil serenity of azure dreams.


Beneath the canvas of azure hue,
Where dreams take flight, where hopes renew,
Above the world, where the larks arise,
Lies the boundless beauty of blue skies.

Infinite vastness, a tranquil sea,
Where wispy clouds dance in symphony,
Whispers of cotton, they softly glide,
In the endless expanse, they confide.
The End
